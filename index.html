Greek Mythology: Home of the gods

Greek mythology, with gods, heroes, and monsters, explores love, power, and tragedy, shaping Western culture's art and literature.

Some random information

Zeus: Supreme ruler of the gods, embodies authority and lightning power.

Aphrodite: Goddess of love, beauty, and desire, influencing hearts and relationships.

Poseidon: God of the sea, earthquakes, storms, and horses.

Ares: God of war, representing the brutal and violent aspects of battle.
